Carbonless Community: Providing you with choices you value.

Carbonless Community's founders are seasoned energy professionals with decades of energy efficiency and commodity procurement experience, who are dedicated to promoting sustainability and helping to create a cleaner earth.

The first product we developed was a personal carbon footprint manager--web and app-based--to help university and corporate communities improve their environmental impact. Our next step in providing pro-environmental products to our clients was to offer carbon offsets. In seeking a more cost-effective way to secure these offsets, we developed an online reverse auction platform.

Finally, we decided to integrate this technology with our background in energy consulting by using our auction platform for electricity and natural gas procurement.
