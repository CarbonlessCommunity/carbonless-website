Special Offer for AASHE members

Carbonless Community is offering a special sustainability opportunity to fellow members of the Association for the Advancement of Sustainability in Higher Education (AASHE). If your organization secures auction services with Carbonless Community for carbon offsets, electricity supply or a combination of both, we will provide a four-week competition at your school with our CC Tracker mobile App.

Our App helps monitor and quantify personal carbon-reducing measures for each participant. Through a combination of competition and cooperation, CC Tracker encourages participants to take more pro-environmental actions in their daily lives.

Our App is a year old and has undergone beta testing at DePaul University last spring. However, we are continually working to improve our product and individuals participating in a Carbonless Community competition can offer constructive improvements, which will be noted on our website with appropriate credit given. To take advantage of this special offer, please contact us and mention AASHE.
