Market for Carbon Offsets and Renewable Energy Certificates

Through the use of the online reverse auction platform, Carbonless Community provides Carbon Offsets and Renewable Energy Certificates, and the associated environmental benefits, in two ways:

  • Our auction can be run with multiple offset providers, bidding in real time to provide you with the lowest cost, certified offsets; and
  • The auction can be conducted with electricity supply backed by Renewable Energy Certificates, thereby providing you with green power.

(Note: our recent experience shows that auction savings are greater than the incremental cost of purchasing RECs. By combing the online reverse auction with electricity backed by RECs, you get an upgrade to green power for free.
