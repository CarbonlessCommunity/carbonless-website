Carbonless Community -- a collection of groups sharing common characteristics or interests, distinct from the larger society, working cooperatively to reduce greenhouse gas emissions, thereby reducing the impact on our planet.









"Reducing greenhouse gas emissions, most notably carbon dioxide (CO2), does not have to be difficult nor costly. The mission of Carbonless Community is to assist groups of like-minded people in executing easy and thoughtful measures that not only reduce their personal carbon footprints, but also reduce the carbon footprint of the larger community to which they belong."

Craig Schuttenberg - CEO of Carbonless Community
