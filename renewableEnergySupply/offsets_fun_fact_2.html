Carbon Offsets 3 (for offsetting Scopes 1, 2 & 3)
RECs 1 (for only Scope 2)



Carbon Offsets can be used to mitigate/offset any greenhouse gas (GHG) emission from your facility. Unlike Renewable Energy Certificates, which can only be used to “...lower an organization’s gross market-based scope 2 emissions from purchased electricity…” Carbon Offsets can be used to “..reduce or offset an organization’s scope 1, 2 or 3 emissions..”1

1. Offsets and RECs: What’s the Difference, EPA Green Power Partnership, February 2018; https://www.epa.gov/sites/default/files/2018- 03/documents/gpp_guide_recs_offsets.pdf

As we reassess the response to the proceedings of COP 26, carbon mitigation strategies will include an ever-increasing role for offsets.
