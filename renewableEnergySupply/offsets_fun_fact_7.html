Carbon Offset Fun Fact #7


By Carbonless Community


Four out of five tonnes of avoided carbon are mitigated by Carbon Offsets


Out of the over 800 institutions of higher education that have signed the Presidents’ Climate Commitment, only 10 have announced carbon neutrality. How did they do it? After exhausting the potential of onsite energy reduction, exploiting the carbon reducing potential of institution-owned land sequestration and installing onsite renewable energy generation, these environmentally-minded institutions depended on some form of offsets – and carbon offsets were utilized four-to-one over RECs.
