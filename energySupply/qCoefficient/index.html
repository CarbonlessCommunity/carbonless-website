QCoefficient – For those choosing to purchase power from a renewable energy generator, QCoefficient’s technology could be most helpful.

Contracts from renewable generators don’t typically guarantee or specify when you will receive your electricity. As electricity is a real-time commodity (with a price that changes constantly throughout the day), you don’t know what your ultimate cost we be to operate your facility. QCoefficient provides the technology and structure to use the thermal mass of your building(s) to vary consumption over short periods to minimize electricity price risk. QCoefficient
